# V-Med ID | Project Features

V-Med ID is an integrated healthcare ecosystem designed to connect patients, doctors, and government bodies through a secure, AI-powered health identity platform.

---

## 1. Patient Dashboard
The patient-facing portal focuses on personal health management, emergency response, and clinical history.

### 1.1 Core Health Management
*   **Vitals Overview (Home)**: A high-level view of current health status, recent vitals, and a "Health Score" calculated based on user data.
*   **Vitals Tracking**: Detailed logs and historical data for Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, SpO2, and Temperature.
*   **Medical Documents**: A secure vault for uploading and managing prescriptions, lab reports, and medical scans.
*   **Medication Manager**: Tracking of active medications and dosages.
*   **Visit History**: Chronological log of previous doctor consultations and clinical visits.

### 1.2 SOS & Emergency Features
*   **Emergency SOS**: A one-tap trigger to alert emergency contacts and family members with real-time location data.
*   **Family Link**: Connect with family members to share health alerts and monitor the status of loved ones.
*   **Blood Donor Network**: 
    *   **Donor Search**: Find nearby blood donors by blood group.
    *   **Urgent Broadcast**: Send immediate alerts to all matching donors within a 10-20km radius.
    *   **Volunteer Registry**: Patients can toggle their availability to become a life-saving blood donor.

### 1.3 AI & Intelligence
*   **AI Health Assistant**: A conversational interface for symptom checking, health advice, and explaining medical scores.
*   **Health Insights**: AI-generated summaries of vitals trends and early warning signals.

### 1.4 Personalization & Localization
*   **Multilingual Support**: Fully localized interface available in **English**, **Hindi** and **Telugu**.
*   **Secure Settings**: Role-based profiles with V-Med ID card generation and biometric/face authentication setups.

---

## 2. Doctor Dashboard
A streamlined clinical interface for healthcare professionals to manage patient care efficiently.

### 2.1 Patient Management
*   **Patient Registry**: A searchable database of all patients registered under the V-Med ID system.
*   **Patient Detail View**: 
    *   Comprehensive clinical history.
    *   Access to patient vitals, historical documents, and AI health summaries.
*   **Add Patient**: Register new patients quickly into the local or global database.

### 2.2 Clinical Workflow
*   **Consultation Tool**: A workflow to manage active patient visits, record clinical notes, and issue digital prescriptions.
*   **Document Management**: Directly upload test results or reports to a patient's digital record.
*   **AI Clinical Support**: AI insights to assist doctors in diagnosing trends or identifying critical anomalies in patient history.

---

## 3. Government Dashboard
A high-level oversight portal for public health analytics and system monitoring.

### 3.1 Analytics & Registry
*   **Population Health Overview**: Aggregated statistics on total users, patient-to-doctor ratios, and daily registration trends.
*   **Global User Registry**: Auditing capabilities to view and search across the entire registry of patients and doctors.

### 3.2 System Integrity
*   **Service Status Monitoring**: Real-time status of critical infrastructure including Firebase Auth, Firestore Database, and AI Backend services.
*   **Registration Tracking**: Monitoring system growth and usage patterns.
